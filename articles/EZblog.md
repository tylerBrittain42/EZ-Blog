EZblog started with a fascination into the personal blog. The idea of each of us maintaining our own space while still remaining connecting. Through this exploration I found myself asking what's to stop the average person and landed on the conclusion: "effort". Heck, I work with computers, but I still haven't set up a blog. So with this, I set about building a simple blog with the idea of just jotting into any sort of file, dropping it into a folder and you are good to go. And that's what this is, the proof of concept of EZblog. Now, I'll be making a foray into working with C, but I'll come back to writing version 1.0 of EZBlog soon enough. While this has been a fun project, I am aware that it is far from perfect and a proof of concept in the most minimal sense.
